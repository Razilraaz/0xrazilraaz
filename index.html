About Me

Hi, I’m Razil Raaz, a passionate penetration tester with a deep interest in cybersecurity and ethical hacking. My goal is to help businesses identify and address security vulnerabilities before malicious actors can exploit them. With a strong background in penetration testing, vulnerability assessments, and security audits, I specialize in uncovering weaknesses in networks, applications, and systems. I thrive in challenging environments and am dedicated to staying up-to-date with the latest security trends, tools, and techniques. I approach each project with a methodical mindset, combining technical expertise and ethical practices to provide actionable insights and recommendations that enhance an organization’s security posture. Proficient in tools like Metasploit, Burp Suite, and Nessus, with a strong foundation in ethical hacking and cybersecurity best practices.
